Pro: The pro offense is primarily for the coach who wants to prepare his players for the next level by using an offense that closely resembles those used in the National Football League. This roster includes a balanced number of players at each position.
Spread: The spread offense is perfect for an innovative coach who primarly wants to use a shotgun formation and lots of wide receivers to stretch the defense out horizontally. This roster has an increased number of players at the reciever position and does not need any fullbacks.
Option: The option offense is the ideal choice for the old school coach looking to bring 3 yards and a cloud of dust back to college football. This roster requires an increased number of quarterbacks and runningbacks, but no tight ends.
